Morpheus8

Stretch-mark removal treatment

Stretch marks are common among women who have undergone pregnancy (especially during the last trimester), experienced rapid weight gain/loss or have a family history of stretch marks. While it poses no health risks, it is a cosmetic concern for many, which can affect self esteem. Morpheus8 - a non-surgical, painless treatment - is a viable option for many women who want to experience long-lasting, fresh and youthful skin. It essentially uses microneedling technology that emits radiofrequency energy to create tiny injuries for the skin to repair itself.

What is the
Morpheus8 treatment?

Morpheus8 is a microneedling treatment that assists in generating collagen by penetrating the middle layers of the skin with radiofrequency energy. It leverages on the Burst Technology which delivers bipolar radiofrrequency energy at three different levels within the skin layers to remodel them within a single cycle, which reduces the treatment time needed. It is also the only Food and Drug Administration (FDA) authorised treatment that allows a penetration of 8mm deep within the adipose layer of the skin, to deeply nourish the skin and solve the problem of stretch marks from its roots. It is a non-surgical, painless method to treat stretch marks, making it a good option for stretch mark removal.

How does
it work?

Morpheus8 is a mircroneedling treatment that penetrates into skin wth radiofrequency energy. An advanced derma roller device is used to create tiny injuries in the skin, which triggers a wound healing process that smoothens the textured stretch marks. Parts of the body where this treatment can be done: face, neck, stomach, upper arms, thighs, buttocks, bra rolls, under the chin, under the eyes and breasts.

Why choose
Morpheus8?

Morpheus8 is a non-surgical method to remove stretch marks, making it a tempting option for many. Together with removal of stretch marks, customers can enjoy other benefits such as tightening and lightening of the skin, removal of acne scars as well as pimples and wrinkes. The effects of the treatment can be seen within 3-4 days of taking the treatment. The results continue to improve over the course of the year with the production of new collagen.

Candidates for treatment

The Morpheus8 treatment is recommended for any women who experience stretch marks, be it young or old. However, candidates below 18 are not allowed to take this treatment and candidates below 21 years old require parental consent. Women who have more active skin infections, are pregnant or breastfeeding should delay taking the Morpheus8 treatment till they have consulted a doctor.
