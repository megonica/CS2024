Laser Hair Removal

Remove the unwanted hairs

We use a dual functional laser to remove all the unwanted hair around the body, it is a long lasting procedure, making sure your skin feels silky smooth without the hairs.

Utilizing the laser's 2 wavelengths to target the hair on the body without the surrounding skin cells by having the hair follicle absorb the light, thus burning away the hair while leaving the skin intact.

What is the Laser Hair Removal treatment?

People who wants to get rid of their body hair, either finding it a nuisance to shave or usually suffers from razor burns or "chicken skin". This procedure works with both men and women, with all types of skin colours and we can remove the hair from the face, arms, hands, torso, back, legs and feet. This procedure is mostly painless and will help you remove the hair to ensure a smooth skin.

How does it work?

The procedure is handled with a dual wavelength laser, an alexandrite laser firing at 755nm wavelength to target the exposed hair as its power level is quite low and diode laser firing at 808nm wavelength to target the more rooted hair follicle, both laser are designed to burn off the hair follicle with a concentrated energy beam while keeping the surrounding skin cells intact and unharmed. We will first apply a topical anesthetic to reduce the discomfort caused by the laser. The procedure can be permenant and the number of sessions depends on how much hair you want permenantly removed.

Why choose Laser Hair Removal?

Compared to other versions of hair removal, the laser version is more effective compared to Intense Pulse Light, needing lesser sessions to get better results. It is also faster to complete a session with laser, with it being 2 to 3 times faster than IPL. It also generate less pain than IPL with laser being slightly more expensive than IPL.

Candidates for treatment

Laser Hair Removal treatment is recommended for:

  • People who dislike shaving
  • People who suffered razor burn when shaving
  • Both men and women

It is advisable not to proceed with the treatment if you had skin cancer, are taking acne medicine or have keloid scars.
