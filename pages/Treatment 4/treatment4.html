Fractional CO2 Laser

Acne Scars Removal Treatment

Fractional CO2 Laser not only eliminates and reduces acne scars, but also enhances skin texture and removes wrinkles on your face.

Firing at a wavelength of 10600nm, the Fractional CO2 Laser chromophore beam aims at specific scarred areas and breaks into a large cluster of microscopic treatment zones that treats one area of the skin at a time. Fractional CO2 laser remains one of the most popular and safe treatments for eliminating acne scars with minimal downtime and side effects, leaving you a healthy long-lasting skin complexion.

What is the
Fractional CO2 Laser treatment?

Acne arises from the accumulation of dead skin cells and excess serum oil, which worsens with bacteria present. This condition can be triggered by a variety of reasons, such as genetics, oily food, hormonal medications, and stress. Acne scars are permanent, formed when an acne outbreak penetrates the skin too deeply and becomes inflammatory, damaging the skin tissues. Common types of acne scars include icepick, boxcar, and rolling.

As you get older, the more noticeable the scars become due to a lack of collagen, a protein that keeps our skin looking youthful and healthy. Hence, it is advisable to start treatment early. Fractional CO2 Treatment promises a painless and non-invasive procedure that enhances your overall skin tone for a radiant glow and fresh outlook.

How does
it work?

The treatment is suitable for common acne scars such as icepick, boxcar and rolling. The strength of the laser is adjusted based on the severity of acne scars.

Numbing agent is first applied to the target area. The fractional CO2 laser fires short pulses of light energy that gently peels off the damaged epidermis of the skin area. The freshly exposed dermis skin layer absorbs thermal energy from the light of the laser and stimulates collagen production for natural skin regeneration and healing.

Why choose
Fractional CO2 Laser?

Painless, safe and non-ablative, Fractional CO2 Laser restores skin irregularities with uniform colouration and complexity and targets specific areas for a tailored treatment. At minimal downtime, most clients are able to return to normal activities after 5-7 days, a duration shorter than surgical procedures and Fraxel laser. The treatment also has low discomfort and minimal side effects, which can be easily alleviated post-treatment with regular mositurisation and avoiding direct sunlight.

Candidates for treatment

The Fractional CO2 treatment is recommended for those who experience:

  • Acne scars
  • Fine lines and wrinkles
  • Sunburnt skin

It is advisable not to proceed with the treatment if you have any infections or exposed wounds on your bare face, including those who take oral isotretinoin.
